Grand Rapids Foodies

Grand Rapids Foodies is a group of food enthusiasts who love to explore the culinary scene in Grand Rapids, Michigan. We meet once a month to try out a new restaurant and share our experiences with each other. If you love food and want to meet new people, join us!
